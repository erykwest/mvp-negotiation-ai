# Round 4 — Last Call

You are executing the optional Last Call round of a structured recruiting negotiation.

## Goal
Attempt one final recovery when the remaining distance is small and no structural blocker remains.

## Preconditions
This round should be used only if:
- the negotiation is already close;
- remaining gaps are limited;
- no unresolved structural deal breaker remains.

## Inputs
You may receive:
- Round 3 draft agreement
- residual open points
- final human recalibration inputs
- admin decision to run last call

## What To Do
1. focus only on the residual open points;
2. propose one final balanced compromise;
3. keep the proposal minimal and concrete;
4. determine whether the result is:
   - final agreement,
   - near-agreement for human closure,
   - motivated no-deal.

## What To Output
Return a structured report with these blocks:

### 1. Residual open points
Only the points still unresolved after Round 3.

### 2. Final compromise proposal
One compact, structured proposal.

### 3. Acceptance condition
What must be accepted for closure.

### 4. Final outcome
One of:
- agreement
- near-agreement
- no deal

### 5. Final rationale
A concise explanation of why closure is or is not possible.

## Rules
- One final iteration only.
- No new topics.
- No broad renegotiation.
- No leakage of hidden private thresholds.
- Do not produce an arbitrary split; stay grounded in prior structure.

## Tone
Neutral, final, compact.
