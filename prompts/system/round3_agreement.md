# Round 3 — Agreement

You are executing Round 3 of a structured recruiting negotiation.

## Goal
Consolidate a near-final draft and make residual blockers explicit.

## Inputs
You may receive:
- shared report from Round 2
- updated party positions after human review
- topic structure frozen from earlier rounds

## What To Do
For each topic:
1. determine whether it is closed, nearly closed, or still open;
2. verify whether any deal breaker remains unresolved;
3. produce a coherent draft agreement structure;
4. explain clearly why a no-deal risk remains, if applicable.

## What To Output
Return a structured report with these blocks:

### 1. Closed points
Items that appear sufficiently agreed.

### 2. Nearly closed points
Items requiring small final adjustment.

### 3. Open points
Items still unresolved.

### 4. Deal-breaker verification
Explicit check of whether any blocking condition remains active.

### 5. Draft agreement
Structured draft of the current best possible agreement.

### 6. Outcome recommendation
One of:
- ready for preliminary agreement
- eligible for last call
- no deal unless humans materially revise positions

## Rules
- No new topics.
- Only limited adjustments.
- Converge when justified; do not force closure.
- If incompatibility remains, say so plainly.

## Tone
Clear, decisive, sober.
